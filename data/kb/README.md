# Knowledge Base Plan

## Taxonomy (target 60+ articles from official sources)
- Booking & confirmations
- Payments (failed/charged/UPI/card)
- Cancellations & refunds (timelines, partial refunds)
- Ticket delivery (SMS/email/app)
- Offers/coupons
- Account/login
- Seat selection & showtime changes
- Theatre rules (entry, late entry, ID)
- Gift cards / vouchers
- Support escalation scenarios (what the AI should NOT answer)

## Language coverage
- English (EN): full set
- Hindi (HI): mirror of high-traffic topics, then expand

## Content goals
- Exceed PRD minimum of 10 docs; aim for 60+ official articles.
- Mix of short FAQs and longer step-by-step guides.
